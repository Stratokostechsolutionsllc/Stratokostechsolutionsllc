How I work

A framework, not a formula

Every engagement is different. But the way I think about the work is consistent — diagnose before prescribing, measure what matters, and leave teams stronger than I found them.

01 · Diagnose

Understand before prescribing

Spend the first week in listening mode. Talk to stakeholders, review the backlog, sit in ceremonies. No recommendations until the actual problem is clear — not the stated one.

02 · Focus

Pick the right lever

Most team problems have one root cause driving ten symptoms. Find that cause — whether it's unclear ownership, missing data, or broken stakeholder trust — and fix it first.

03 · Deliver

Move fast with discipline

Bias toward action. Ship small, measure early, iterate. Every output — roadmap, sprint, training — is tied to a measurable outcome so we know if it's working.

04 · Enable

Build lasting capability

The goal is a team that doesn't need me. Every engagement ends with documentation, handoff, and coaching so improvements compound after I'm gone.
